Government Circulations for Farmers

Stay updated with the latest official announcements, schemes, and advisories released by the government for the benefit of farmers.

Weather Alert: Heavy Rainfall Expected

IMD Advisory | Next 48 Hours

Heavy to very heavy rainfall is expected in several agricultural regions. Farmers are advised to postpone harvesting activities and ensure proper drainage to prevent waterlogging in fields.

Market Rate Increase: Soybean & Cotton

Agricultural Market Update

The market price of Soybean and Cotton has increased by 6–8% in major mandis. Farmers planning to sell are advised to check nearby market rates for better returns.

Weather Advisory: Heatwave Alert

IMD Warning

A heatwave is likely to affect crop growth in the coming days. Farmers are advised to increase irrigation frequency and avoid daytime spraying of fertilizers.

Market Rate Decrease: Onion Prices Drop

Market Intelligence Report

Onion prices have decreased due to increased supply in major markets. Farmers are advised to store produce safely or delay selling if possible.

Pest Alert: Fall Armyworm Detected

Crop Protection Advisory

Fall Armyworm infestation has been reported in maize-growing regions. Farmers are advised to monitor crops closely and follow recommended control measures.

Weather Alert: Frost Risk in Northern Regions

Seasonal Weather Update

There is a risk of frost during early morning hours. Farmers should use protective irrigation methods and cover sensitive crops to minimize damage.

PM-KISAN Scheme – Installment Release

Issued by Ministry of Agriculture | Feb 2026

The next installment under the PM-KISAN scheme will be credited directly to eligible farmers’ bank accounts. Farmers are advised to verify their Aadhaar and bank details to avoid delays.

Crop Insurance Enrollment Deadline

Issued by Government of India

Farmers are informed that the last date to enroll in the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) for the upcoming season is approaching. Ensure timely registration to secure crop insurance benefits.

Subsidy on Drip & Sprinkler Irrigation

Issued by State Agriculture Department

A new subsidy has been announced for farmers adopting micro-irrigation systems such as drip and sprinkler irrigation to promote water conservation and increase crop productivity.

Soil Health Card Update Notice

Issued by Department of Agriculture

Farmers are requested to update their Soil Health Card details at the nearest agriculture office. Updated soil reports will help in better crop planning and fertilizer usage.
